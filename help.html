How to Use the Site

Welcome to the Gig E-Commerce site! Here’s a quick guide on how to use the site:

  1. Browse through the available gigs listed on the homepage.
  2. Click on the “Order” button next to a gig to place an order.
  3. You will be redirected to my Facebook profile.
  4. Chat to compelete the order.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact me on Facebook.
